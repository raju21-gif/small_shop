üìÑ Abstract

ShopManager AI is a comprehensive full-stack web application designed to modernize small business operations through intelligent automation. Built with a robust FastAPI (Python) backend and a reactive JavaScript/TailwindCSS frontend, the system bridges the gap between traditional inventory management and modern e-commerce.

The core innovation lies in its dual-facing architecture: an Admin Dashboard for business owners to manage stock, visualize sales trends, and leverage AI-driven demand forecasting, and a Customer Marketplace where users can browse real-time inventory and place purchase requests. The application features a secure, role-based approval workflow, ensuring that high-value transactions are verified before stock is deducted. By integrating machine learning for stock prediction and real-time synchronization between inventory and sales channels, ShopManager AI minimizes stockouts, prevents overstocking, and streamlines the entire retail lifecycle.
